Rooted Alpha Pilot · Last updated May 2026

1
Who we are
Rooted is an independent research project founded by Jenny Yiqing Zhao, currently in pre-product development. This pilot is conducted solely for research purposes and is not affiliated with any regulated financial institution.
2
This is a simulated prototype
This 30-day pilot ("the Alpha") is strictly an early-stage market research exercise. There are no real financial transactions, no money is accepted, and we will never ask for your bank or card details. All balances, spending logs, and activity data are entirely simulated.
3
Not a financial product (FCA disclaimer)
Rooted is a research project, not a bank or regulated financial institution. This Alpha is not regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) of the United Kingdom. No part of this simulation constitutes financial advice, a financial promotion, or an offer of regulated payment services.
4
Simulated features & placeholder content
To demonstrate how Rooted will work at scale, this prototype uses the following placeholder content:
  • Energy balances are manually assigned by the research team and carry no real-world monetary value
  • Friend profiles in the Grove feature are pre-populated placeholders to simulate the social and co-planting experience. They do not represent real users
  • Partner brand activations are illustrative and do not represent live commercial agreements
  • Environmental and forest content — including mapping and video — is used for illustrative purposes only, and may include third-party material such as content from Ecosia. This does not imply Rooted ownership of or involvement in those specific projects
  • No trees are planted during this pilot. Reforestation is the intended outcome of the live product, not this research prototype
5
Your data (UK GDPR)
We collect only your name, email address, and in-app activity — including login timestamps, feature interactions, and check-in responses — to conduct this research. Your data is strictly confidential, will never be sold or shared with any third party, and will be retained until the official product launch or a maximum of 3 years from the date of this pilot, whichever comes first. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.
6
Your pilot energy carries over
The energy balance you accumulate during this pilot will be carried over to your official Rooted account when the product launches, provided you register with the same email address. This is our way of recognising your contribution as a founding member of the Rooted community.
This is our current intention. We cannot guarantee this transfer in the event of a material change to the product or business model.
7
Your rights
You can withdraw from the pilot at any time without consequence. On withdrawal, your in-app data will be deleted within 7 days upon request. Your right to withdraw does not affect anything already collected prior to withdrawal.
