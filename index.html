How it works

Three habits, one tree.

🚶

Move

TfL taps and daily steps earn credits automatically. Your commute counts toward something permanent.

🛍

Spend consciously

Card spending at ethical, B Corp certified brands earns credits toward your tree.

🌿

Grow together

Co-plant with friends in a Grove. Your tree grows faster. Theirs does too.

Not a token. Not an offset. A real tree, in the ground, tracked to its location.

The tree is the whole point.
